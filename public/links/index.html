Links

This may have once been called a "web directory." What follows is a set of links to pages around the web that I find interesting.

My Sites

  • Iron Arachne - my primary hobby project.
  • Tilde Town - my "tilde" website, ~sithlord, where I post random experiments and toys.
  • Tabletop Role-Playing Game Generators - a web directory I maintain for TTRPG content generators.
  • dungeonHack - my gemlog about gaming. If you don't have a Gemini browser yet, I recommend Lagrange.

Typography and Fonts

  • The Fell Types - about the Fell typefaces, and Igino Marini's digital versions of them
  • Butterick's Practical Typography — a definitive guide to typography
  • Fantasque Sans — my current coding font family
  • Fira Code
  • Iosevka

Food and Drink

  • Supercook — a website that recommends recipes based on your ingredients
  • Webtender — a mixology site, including a cool feature that gives you recipes based on what you have in your bar

Procedural Generation

  • Algorithms for Procedural Content Generation — a bunch of algorithms
  • Here Dragons Abound — a blog by a procedural map generator creator
  • Red Blob Games — tutorials and interactive lessons on procedural generation, especially for games

Tabletop Role-Playing Games

  • Forbidden Lands — a dark fantasy RPG with gorgeous production values
  • Shadow of the Demon Lord — a really dark fantasy RPG with tons of content
  • Uncharted Worlds — a freeform space opera RPG
