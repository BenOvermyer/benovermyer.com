Hi, I'm Ben Overmyer. I love making awesome web experiences for people and machines.

Right now, I help guard the free flow of information as the Digital Manager of Operations at the Minneapolis Star Tribune. Prior to joining the Star Tribune, I worked mostly as a web developer and occasionally a designer.

I lead a team of engineers in building performant web systems that can scale to tens of thousands of concurrent users. Guided by the philosophy of DevOps, we encourage communication and collaboration throughout the organization as we automate a wonderland of server infrastructure.

Interested in reading more? Read my blog at Pathfinder.
